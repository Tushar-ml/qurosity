**Qurosity** is my personal radar for the fascinating. It's a digital scrapbook for the questions that keep me up at night, the weirdly beautiful threads of logic I find myself pulling, and the ideas that feel too important to ignore.

This isn't a place for dry textbook definitions or 280-character hot takes. Instead, you'll find deep dives, half-baked epiphanies, and honest thinking across a messy, beautiful spectrum: **technology**, **physics**, **AI**, **consciousness**, and whatever lies in the spaces between them.

Every post here starts with a spark: *I didn't understand this yesterday, but I'm going to try to map it out today.*

If your favorite pastime is getting lost in a Wikipedia spiral at 2:00 AM, you're in the right place. Welcome to the rabbit hole.
